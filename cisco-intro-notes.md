# Cisco Introduction to Cybersecurity Notes

## Module 1 - Introduction to Cybersecurity

### What is cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber attacks.

### Types of cyber attacks
- Phishing
- Malware
- Ransomware
- Social engineering

### Important concepts
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

### My notes
- Cybersecurity is important for companies and people.
- Many attacks happen through email and fake websites.
- Organizations need protection for their data.
